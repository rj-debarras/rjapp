# Product Marketing Context

*Last updated: 2026-03-27*

## Product Overview
**One-liner:** Débarras Pro IDF provides premium, empathetic debris and estate clearing services, functioning as a "Digital Concierge".
**What it does:** Complete property clearance including handling valuable items and potentially dangerous accumulations (Syndrome de Diogène). It removes the physical and mental burden of clearing a home.
**Product category:** Estate Clearing / Debris Removal
**Product type:** Service
**Business model:** Quoted service, with potential for "Débarras Rémunéré" (paid clearing if valuables exceed clearing cost).

## Target Audience
**Target companies:** Individuals (B2C) and Estate Executors
**Decision-makers:** Homeowners, relatives of the deceased, or individuals overwhelmed by property accumulation.
**Primary use case:** Moving, estate settling after a death, or clearing an unsafe environment (Diogène).
**Jobs to be done:**
- Clear an estate quickly so it can be sold or rented.
- Handle hoarding situations with discretion and no judgment.
- Provide a simple estimation and rapid execution.

## Personas
| Persona | Cares about | Challenge | Value we promise |
|---------|-------------|-----------|------------------|
| Empathy Seeker (Family) | Respect and careful handling | Overwhelmed by volume, emotionally attached | Handled delicately without judgment |
| The Pragmatist (Executor) | Speed and clear pricing | Emptyting to list for sale | Quick, transparent estimation & execution |

## Customer Language
**Words to use:** Accompaniment, Discretion, Evaluation transparent, Valorisation, Respect.
**Words to avoid:** Garbage, Dump, Cheap, Trash.
